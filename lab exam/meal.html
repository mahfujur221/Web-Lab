Grilled Sandwich

Pasta Alfredo

  • Pasta
  • Cream
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan

1. Boil pasta and drain.
2. Sauté garlic in butter.
3. Add cream and cheese.
4. Combine with pasta and serve hot.

Vegetable Fried Rice

  • Cooked Rice
  • Carrots
  • Green Peas
  • Soy Sauce

1. Sauté vegetables in oil.
2. Add rice and soy sauce.
3. Stir-fry everything for 3–4 minutes.
4. Serve warm.
